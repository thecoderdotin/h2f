The Highway to Freedom

RL Stevenson once said -- "For my part, I travel not to go anywhere, but to go. I travel for travelâ€™s sake. The great affair is to move."

It holds true. Both of us - Rahul Ramesh and Vijay S Paul have been "Travel Buddies" for above 2 years now. Rahul on his Royal Enfield and Vijay on his Yamaha FZ-S. Not all our travels are far and away, across the seven seas or over the empowering mountains. Some are short in distance, some short in experience; but every ride has a story to be told.

Our first ride together began in 2013 to Munnar. And since then, we haven't stopped shifting our gears, or let the engine cool down. Every time we rode, we would create a story. A story of people, places, food, experience and whatever the heavens brought our way.

And thus, after requests from various corners dropped down to combine our experiences in writing, we finally decided to officially start "Highway to Freedom". We promise you, it's not going to be just another travelogue. It's a long never ending story which begins with the first kick of the starter. You can smell the air we breathe, hear the music we enjoy, experience the highways we travel on and see what our eyes see.

Because the mind might be trapped by the dogmas surrounding around, but the soul is not. The never ending highways have a story to tell, and we are explorers waiting to write them.
